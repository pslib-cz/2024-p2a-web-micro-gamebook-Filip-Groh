Gamebook - Tell tree about the quest

The whispers subside, replaced by a sense of calm. You feel a gentle pressure, guiding you towards the base of one of the trees. There, nestled amongst the roots, you find a small, shimmering chest.

You open the chest. Inside, nestled on a bed of soft moss, lies the Sunstone. It radiates a warm, golden light, illuminating the clearing. You have succeeded in your quest!

Choose your path:

  • Return to the village: Take the Sunstone back to the village and share your riches with the people.
  • Explore further: Continue exploring the Whispering Woods, eager to uncover more of its secrets.
