Gamebook - Follow statue

As you approach the statue, you hear a mischievous giggle. A swarm of tiny, iridescent sprites emerges from the surrounding bushes, their eyes twinkling with mischief. They circle you, their laughter echoing through the woods.

Choose your action:

  • Offer them a gift: You rummage through your backpack and offer the sprites a handful of berries.
  • Try to scare them away: You shout and wave your arms, hoping to frighten the sprites away.
