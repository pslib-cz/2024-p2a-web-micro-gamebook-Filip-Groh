Gamebook - Tree approach

As you get closer to the trees, the whispers intensify. They seem to come from within the trees themselves, swirling around you like a ghostly mist. You feel a strange tingling sensation, as if the trees are observing you.

Choose your action:

  • Touch one of the trees: You reach out and gently touch the bark of one of the trees.
  • Circle the trees three times: You follow the ancient ritual described in your map, circling the trees three times.
