Gamebook - Sunstone wish

The sprites exchange mischievous glances. "The Sunstone," the largest one replies, "is guarded by the Whispering Trees."

"The Whispering Trees?" you ask, confused.

"They stand at the heart of the woods," the sprite explains. "They whisper secrets, but only to those who are worthy."

The sprites then vanish into the undergrowth, leaving you alone with the knowledge of the Sunstone's location. You continue deeper into the woods, the Whispering Trees now your ultimate goal.

Choose your action:

  • Press on: You continue following the map, determined to reach the Whispering Trees.
  • Rest: You decide to rest for the night, hoping to regain your strength for the journey ahead.
