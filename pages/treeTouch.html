Gamebook - Tree touch

The moment your hand touches the bark, a jolt of energy surges through you. The whispers become louder, more insistent. They seem to be asking you questions, demanding answers.

Choose your action:

  • Tell the trees about your quest: You explain to the trees that you seek the Sunstone.
  • Tell the trees about your fears: You confess your fears about the dangers of the woods.
